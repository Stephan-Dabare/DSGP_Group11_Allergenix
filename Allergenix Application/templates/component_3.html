Why Analyze Cosmetic Ingredients?


Analyzing cosmetic ingredients is essential for identifying potential allergens, harmful chemicals, and ensuring product safety. This knowledge empowers you to choose the right products for your skin, avoid unwanted reactions, and make informed decisions about what you apply. By understanding ingredient compositions, you can prioritize both safety and effectiveness in your skincare routine.

Photo Cropping Instructions

  • Zoom in and crop out everything except the list of ingredients.
  • If the list features "active ingredients," crop and analyze those separately.

Examples of a Good Photo

  • Zoom in
  • Sharp focus
  • Flat surface
  • Straight and well cropped
  • Bright lighting

Examples of a Bad Photo

  • Blurred
  • Not flat
  • Not straight
  • Low contrast
  • Dim lighting
